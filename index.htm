Details

The Rupe Scultura 400 Road Bike is ready to roll out the door day-in day-out for fun, fitness or even racing.  You get the same great Scultura Lite Alloy frame which gets better and better every year, paired with a full CF2 Carbon Fork for confidence-inspiring control and steering. The 400 is equipped with a 2x11 speed Shimano 105 Drivetrain which is performance oriented, right in that sweet spot for the recreational rider that wants to go fast or the experienced rider that just wants a workhorse to get the job done. The Shimano 105 Rim Brakes provide simple and efficient braking and are a bit cheaper than the disc brake model which is a nice added bonus. All-around this bike provides great value, it is a decent step up in quality from the 2X10 Speed Shimano Tiagra on the Scultura 300 and is the highest quality alloy frame Scultura. The Merida Scultura 400 combines a quality alloy frame/carbon fork combination with Shimano 105 and classic rim brakes for a bike that is well-rounded and ready for any road ride.
